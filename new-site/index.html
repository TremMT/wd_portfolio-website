Hi, I'm Tremaine Trotter!

I'm a Front End Web Developer with a passion for creating clean, responsive websites.

I was always interested in computers, the World Wide Web, and the websites that existed there.

I decided to go into web development because I have always been interested in HOW websites were created and imagined creating my own but, as a child, I had convinced myself that creating such spaces was beyond me. What wizardry went into making them, how were they always there, and how were the websites maintained/ changed? Back then, I assumed it was all magic and left it at that. But that interest would remain.

After discovering a career in fashion design was NOT for me, learning how to build computers from scratch, and deciding on a second career change, I decided to try coding to see what magic I could create and share. I am glad I took the plunge! I truly enjoy the ups and downs of becoming and being a web developer and look forward to learning more of the coding languages and finally creating spaces of my own on the World Wide Web.

When I’m not working or coding, I’m most likely playing videos games and doing online research on anything that intrigues me. I also love reading, journaling, and exploring new music.

I look forward to working with you!
